\section*{6. Conclusion and Future Work}
We evaluated AWS Lambda Durable Functions through a counter entity and a video-encoding pipeline.
Results show up to 42\,\% cost reduction and lower latency compared to traditional Lambda + DynamoDB, while preserving strict sequential semantics compatible with the actor model.
Future directions include multi-region workflows, finer-grained cost optimisations, and integration with AI agents that require long-lived stateful conversations.
