\begin{abstract}
AWS Lambda Durable Functions (preview, December 2025) introduce checkpoint-and-replay
primitives that keep state without persistent compute. We evaluate the SDK through a
counter entity and a video-encoding pipeline, and position the service within the
actor-model landscape. Results show up to 42\,\% cost reduction versus external-state
patterns and strict sequential semantics compatible with classical actors.
\end{abstract}
